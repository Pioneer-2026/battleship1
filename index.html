How to play

Place ships, pass the phone, then fire at coordinates. First to sink all opponent ships wins.

Placement

  • Click cells to place the highlighted ship.
  • Use Rotate or Randomize to speed up placement.

Battle

  • Tap enemy grid to fire. Hits are red, misses are blue.
  • When it's the other player's turn, pass the phone and press Continue.
